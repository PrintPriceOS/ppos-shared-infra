# V27 Autonomous Civilizational Layer Spec v1.0.0

## 1. Introduction
The **Autonomous Civilizational Layer / Global Knowledge & Coordination Substrate (V27)** is the layer that enables PrintPrice OS to act as a durable industrial memory. It preserves knowledge, doctrine, and patterns to ensure coordination intelligence compounds over time.

## 2. Core Concepts
- **Ecosystem Memory**: The aggregate of meaningful historical traces across all layers.
- **Operational Doctrine**: Canonical operating guidance derived from evidence-backed lessons.
- **Coordination Intelligence**: Reusable patterns of multi-actor cooperation and problem-solving.
- **Civilizational Continuity**: The preservation of knowledge across organizational and team turnovers.

## 3. Knowledge Taxonomy
Memory objects are structured as:
- `memory_record`: raw evidence.
- `lesson`: an observation from a specific incident.
- `pattern`: a recurring observation.
- `doctrine`: a canonical, versioned operating rule.
- `precedent`: a reference decision with auditable rationale.

## 4. Continuity & Stewardship
- **Stewardship**: Governed roles for curating and canonicalizing knowledge.
- **Retention**: Non-negotiable rules for preserving constitutional and strategic history.
- **Explanation**: Every piece of doctrine must link back to its evidence lineage.

## 5. Learning Compounding
The system moves from reactive governance to proactive doctrinal reasoning, using the precedent library to inform current strategic and operational assessments.
