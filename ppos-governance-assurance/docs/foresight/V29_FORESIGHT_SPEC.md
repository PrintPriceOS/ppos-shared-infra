# V29 Autonomous Strategic Foresight & Evolution Engine Spec v1.0.0

## 1. Introduction
The **Autonomous Strategic Foresight & Evolution Engine (V29)** is the layer that enables PrintPrice OS to act as an anticipatory industrial intelligence system. it detects emerging changes and identifies adaptation paths before the ecosystem is forced to react.

## 2. Core Concepts
- **Weak Signals**: Early, low-intensity indicators of structural change.
- **Structural Trends**: Meaningful, evidence-backed directional movements in the ecosystem.
- **Scenario Foresight**: Construction of multiple plausible futures to guide strategic preparation.
- **Evolution Paths**: Governed adaptation strategies for protocol, doctrine, and governance.

## 3. Foresight Horizons
- `immediate`: 0-30 days (operational focus).
- `near_term`: 1-2 quarters (tactical/strategic focus).
- `medium_term`: 2-6 quarters (evolution focus).
- `long_term`: 6+ quarters (structural focus).

## 4. Legitimacy & Governance
- **Evidence-Linked**: All foresight outputs must be grounded in signals, trends, and historical analogies.
- **Institutional Review**: High-impact evolution recommendations require formal institutional and constitutional review.
- **No Speculation**: Opaque prediction claims detached from auditable evidence are rejected.

## 5. Success Metric
The system moves from reactive learning to proactive orientation, reducing the latency between structural change and governed adaptation.
