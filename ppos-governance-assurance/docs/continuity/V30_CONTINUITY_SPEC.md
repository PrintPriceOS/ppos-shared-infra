# V30 Autonomous Strategic Continuity & Self-Renewal Spec v1.0.0

## 1. Introduction
The **Autonomous Strategic Continuity & Self-Renewal Layer (V30)** is the final layer of the PrintPrice OS roadmap. It ensures that the ecosystem remains viable, coherent, and fresh across long time horizons (decades), protecting it against institutional stagnation, principle drift, and network entropy.

## 2. Core Concepts
- **Institutional Renewal**: Periodic, governed refreshing of standards, doctrine, and governance models.
- **Principle Drift**: The slow deviation of network behavior from core constitutional and standard principles.
- **Ecosystem Entropy**: The accumulation of complexity, fragmentation, and technical/governance debt.
- **Long-Horizon Viability**: The capacity of the network to sustain value and trust across generations of participants.

## 3. Continuity Horizons
- `active_cycle`: 1-2 years (operational renewal).
- `institutional_horizon`: 5-10 years (standard/doctrine renewal).
- `civilizational_horizon`: 20-50+ years (constitutional/structural renewal).

## 4. Renewal Pillars
- **Doctrine Freshness**: Ensuring operational guidance remains relevant and evidence-backed.
- **Governance Agility**: Preventing stagnation in decision-making and institutional stewards.
- **Standard Integrity**: Protecting the FEP and Operating Standards from fragmented evolution.
- **Trust Sustainability**: Preserving trust foundations even as participant cohorts change.

## 5. Exit Criteria (Roadmap Completion)
Completion of V30 marks the transition of PrintPrice OS from a platform to a **durable, self-governing industrial institution**.
