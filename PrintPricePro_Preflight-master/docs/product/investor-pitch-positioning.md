## Slide 5: Market Opportunity — The Automated Publishing Stack
This slide demonstrates the Total Addressable Market (TAM) expansion by placing PrintPrice in the infrastructure layer.

### The Rise of Automated Publishing Platforms
Publishing and printing are moving to automated platforms. Millions of PDFs are generated every day by:
- Self-publishing platforms (e.g., Amazon KDP)
- Web-to-print platforms
- Design tools (e.g., Canva)
- E-commerce product generators
- Automated publishing systems

These PDFs must become **production-ready print files** before they can simply be "printed".

### The Structural Gap
**Current workflow:**
`Design tools → PDF → Human prepress operator → Printing`

Traditional prepress tools were designed for desktop use, human operators, and manual corrections. This model **does not scale to automated platforms**.

### The New Workflow
`Design tools / Platforms`
`       ↓`
**`PrintPrice Preflight (Automation Layer)`**
`       ↓`
`Production-ready PDF`
`       ↓`
`Digital press / printer`

PrintPrice becomes the **infrastructure layer** that prepares files automatically.

### Market Size
| Layer | Market Size |
|-------|-------------|
| Global printing market | ~$850B |
| Digital printing | ~$200B |
| Web-to-print platforms | ~$35B |
| **Automated publishing infrastructure** | **Emerging multi-billion layer** |

The infrastructure that enables automated publishing is still **largely unbuilt**.
