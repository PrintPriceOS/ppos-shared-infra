# Executive One-Pager: PrintPrice OS Industrial Coordination Protocol

## The Mission
PrintPrice OS is the industrial operating system for global publishing. We solve the fundamental fragmentation of the print industry by providing a **Unified Industrial Coordination Protocol** (R1-R12) that digitizes specification, production, finance, and logistics.

## The Problem
Industrial printing is currently manual, localized, and opaque. High-volume publishers lack real-time pricing, while printers suffer from underutilized capacity and slow settlements.

## The Solution: A 13-Layer Industrial Stack
We have engineered a vertically integrated infrastructure that handles the entire production lifecycle:
1.  **Platform Intelligence**: Predictive selection and SLA risk scoring.
2.  **Federated Network**: A zero-trust grid of autonomous production nodes.
3.  **Autonomous Exchange**: Treating printing capacity as a liquid global commodity.
4.  **Financial Settlement**: Production-grade escrow and automated multi-currency payouts.
5.  **Logistics Orchestration**: (Target State) End-to-end physical fulfillment and delivery.

## Why Now?
The shift toward distributed manufacturing and sustainable, local production requires a programmable coordination layer. PrintPrice OS is the infrastructure for this transition.
