# Real-Time Offer Competition — Phase R10

## 1. Dynamic Market Tendering
The exchange facilitates a **live competitive environment** where printers can adjust their bids based on market signals.

### Competition Lifecycle
1. **Initial Bid**: Node submits standard price based on base rate + margin.
2. **Signal Ingress**: Node receives "Market Position" signal (e.g., "Your bid is in the top 30%").
3. **Yield Adjustment**: Node may lower price to secure volume or raise price if capacity becomes scarce.
4. **Final Lock**: Exchange selects the "Best Utility Offer" and awards the contract.

## 2. Competitive Leverages
- **Aggressive Filling**: Small printers offering 20% discounts to fill immediate "Gap Slots".
- **Premium Locking**: Tier-1 printers maintaining price but offering "SLA Insurance" (guaranteed 24h delivery).

## 3. Results
- **Outcome**: Price discovery happens in seconds, not days.
- **Fairness**: Automated scoring ensures that "Price Dumping" is balanced against "Confidence Risk" (R9).
