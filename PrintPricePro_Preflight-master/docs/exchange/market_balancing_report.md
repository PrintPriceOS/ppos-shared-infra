# Market Balancing Report — Phase R10

## 1. Autonomous Equilibrium
The exchange continuously balances **Global Production Weight**.

### Balancing Strategies
| Strategy | Mechanism | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Regional Incentivization**| Automated margin adjustments. | Fill idle capacity in a specific country. |
| **Priority Leveling** | Move low-urgency jobs out of congested hubs. | Protect High-SLA "Urgent" jobs. |
| **Monopoly Prevention** | Caps on total network share per printer. | Ensure supplier diversity and redundancy. |

## 2. Dynamic Redistribution
- **The "Overflow" Logic**: When EU-WEST is at 110% of predicted capacity, the system automatically redirects incoming "Standard" jobs to EU-CENTRAL, sacrificing slight shipping cost for guaranteed production uptime.

## 3. Findings
