# R11 — Production Liquidity Layer Report

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The **Production Liquidity Layer (R11)** is the final bridge between digital coordination and industrial economy. By implementing autonomous settlement, production-grade escrow, and multi-currency liquidity management, the **Global Print Exchange** has matured from a software market into a **Financially Executed Industrial Infrastructure.**

## CORE CAPABILITIES ACTIVE
1. **Production Escrow**: Secure, state-aware funding for industrial jobs.
2. **Autonomous Settlement**: Zero-touch fee capture and payout splitting.
3. **Multi-Currency Liquidity**: Global FX support with rate-locking stability.
4. **Dispute-Aware Flows**: Structured arbitration integrated with financial release.
5. **Printer Payout Infrastructure**: High-velocity payment rails for industrial providers.
6. **Production Credit Layer**: Financial leverage for trusted network participants.
